FAQ

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    2. You need first to go to the HZ print page, log into your account and then you can upload all necessary files you want to print. After choosing file(s) you can set additional settings and confirm your print request. Next, you go to the printer (they are on each floor), scan your card in it, choose printing option and you can enjoy your printed document.

  2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

    3. Scanning a document and sending it to your laptop instructions are available on this page.

  3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

    4. First of all you need to log in and then you can buy whatever you want.

  4. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

    5. All necessary information about COVID and related instructions you can find on this page

  5. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    6. You can book it via SelfService Portal.

  6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    7. You need to reveal you HZ pass at the barriers so as to get into the HZ parking lot and park there.
